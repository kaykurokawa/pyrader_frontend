More Features

Blkdat works by GUI interaction or by taking user inputs from the url query string. In order to intract with the price data you can do the following:

http://blkdat.com/?price&symbol=BTC&unit=USD&exchange=coinbase&interval=5min

The following are the query string parameters:

  • /?price
    your choice of price or block data (required)
  • &symbol
    your choice of instrument symbol (required)
  • &unit
    your choice of units (required)
  • &exchange
    choose your exhchage, if blank it will draw an average of all exchanges (not required)
  • &interval
    your choice of interval, if left blank it will draw from 0 averaging (not required)

Likewise with the block data you can populate it buy:

http://blkdat.com/?block&coin=XMO&datatype=hash_rate&interval=hour
  • /?block
    your choice of price or block data (required)
  • &coin
    your choice of instrument symbol (required)
  • &datatype
    your choice of unis (required)
  • &interval
    your choice of interval, if left blank it will draw from 0 averaging which is a scatter plot. (not required)

Finally you can use a combination of price/volume/block query parameters in combination to display an infinite amount of graphs! 

http://blkdat.com/?price&symbol=BTC&unit=USD&exchange=coinbase/?block&coin=XMO&datatype=difficulty&interval=hour

3. In the Condition select, choose to send notification when >, <, = to condition is met.

4. In the Price/Block Point input the condition value (i.e. when the price of bitcoin is more than 6000)
